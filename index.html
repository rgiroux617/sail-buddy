How to Play

Clear as many of the 30 mines as you can in 60s (45s for the Dualthrust).

Tips for Play

Colliding with land or running over mines will damage the hull unless your shield is activated

The shield is also the brake (and let's you make tighter turns)

Zoom out or use radar to identify mine locations

You get 3 depth charges for mass (and safe) mine collection

There's no way to stop the boat - ain't nobody got time for that!

The depth of your failure will be assessed once you wreck or run out of time.
